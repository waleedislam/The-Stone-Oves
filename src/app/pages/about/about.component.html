How It Started

A Passion Project
Turned Legacy

It started with one stone oven, two recipes, and an unshakeable belief that Faisalabad deserved world-class pizza. Our founder Chef Usman spent years perfecting the dough recipe — a 48-hour cold ferment that gives our crust its unmistakable flavor.

Three years later, The Stone Oves has served over 2,000 customers and launched 50+ menu items — all made fresh daily, all stone-fired to order. No frozen dough. No reheated sauces. Just pure craft.
